How It Works
  1. Enter a YouTube video URL (videos in English)
  2. We download the audio from the video
  3. The audio is transcribed to text using speech recognition
  4. The text is translated to Brazilian Portuguese
  5. A new audio file is generated with the translated speech
  6. The timing is adjusted to match the original audio perfectly
Important: Long videos (over 1 hour) will be processed in chunks and might take longer to translate.
